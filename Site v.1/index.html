Vivek Bigelow

About


Vivek Bigelow is a designer, filmmaker, artist, photographer, writer, and musician. While attending Indiana University, Vivek studied Fine Arts and worked with several mediums including 3D graphics, video, web design, audio, photography, and digital design. Vivek feels that art is a form of communication, a way for one to express the inexpressible. Art is directly intertwined with the human experience, and successful art tells a compelling story. Vivek is interested in using various media to tell diverse and specific narratives. Currently Vivek is working on two short films, a screenplay for a feature film, and recording an album.
